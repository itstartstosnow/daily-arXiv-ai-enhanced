Motivation: 解决Jain等人(ICML 2023)提出的开放问题：展示持续观察模型中遗忘设置与自适应设置之间的分离。持续观察模型形式化了流算法的隐私保护需求，其中数据随时间到达并在每个时间步输出结果。遗忘设置仅要求对预先固定的数据流提供隐私保护，而自适应设置要求即使对基于算法输出自适应选择的数据流也能提供隐私保护。

Method: 基于Bun、Steinke和Ullman(SODA 2017)的相关向量查询问题构建分离。为遗忘设置设计了一个(ε,0)-DP算法，在输入维度上能保持指数多个时间步的准确性。同时证明任何(ε,δ)-DP自适应算法在仅释放常数个时间步的输出后就会失去准确性。

Result: 成功展示了持续观察模型中遗忘设置与自适应设置之间的显式分离。遗忘算法能在指数多个时间步保持准确性，而自适应算法在常数步后即失效，这为两种设置提供了严格的理论界限。

Conclusion: 该研究首次明确分离了持续观察模型中的遗忘设置与自适应设置，解决了Jain等人提出的开放问题。结果表明自适应设置下的隐私保护要求比遗忘设置严格得多，为流算法隐私保护的理论研究提供了重要洞见。

Abstract: We resolve an open question of Jain, Raskhodnikova, Sivakumar, and Smith (ICML 2023) by exhibiting a problem separating differential privacy under continual observation in the oblivious and adaptive settings. The continual observation (a.k.a. continual release) model formalizes privacy for streaming algorithms, where data is received over time and output is released at each time step. In the oblivious setting, privacy need only hold for data streams fixed in advance; in the adaptive setting, privacy is required even for streams that can be chosen adaptively based on the streaming algorithm's output.
  We describe the first explicit separation between the oblivious and adaptive settings. The problem showing this separation is based on the correlated vector queries problem of Bun, Steinke, and Ullman (SODA 2017). Specifically, we present an $(\varepsilon,0)$-DP algorithm for the oblivious setting that remains accurate for exponentially many time steps in the dimension of the input. On the other hand, we show that every $(\varepsilon,δ)$-DP adaptive algorithm fails to be accurate after releasing output for only a constant number of time steps.
